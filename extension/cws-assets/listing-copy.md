### Full Description

**Free Up Memory with Automatic Tab Suspension**

Tab Suspender helps you manage memory across many open tabs. Automatically suspend inactive tabs after a configurable timeout, then instantly restore them when you need them. Perfect for power users who keep dozens of tabs open.

**Key Features:**

✓ **Configurable Timeouts** – Set suspension after 5, 15, 30, or 60 minutes of inactivity
✓ **Smart Whitelists** – Exclude important domains and tab groups from suspension
✓ **Instant Restoration** – Suspended tabs restore instantly with one click
✓ **Keyboard Shortcut** – Alt+S to quickly suspend the current tab
✓ **Right-Click Menu** – Suspend tabs directly from the context menu
✓ **Tab Group Support** – Respects Chrome's tab groups
✓ **Favicon Preservation** – Easily recognize suspended tabs by their icons
✓ **Session Recovery** – Restore your entire tab session on browser restart

**How It Works:**

Tab Suspender monitors your open tabs and suspends those you haven't used recently. Suspended tabs appear grayed out and use almost zero RAM. When you click a suspended tab, it instantly restores with all content intact.

Set your preferred idle timeout in the extension settings:
- 5 minutes: For ultra-aggressive memory management
- 15 minutes: Recommended for most users
- 30 minutes: For users with many active tabs
- 60 minutes: For light memory pressure

**Whitelist Control:**

Add domains or tab groups to your whitelist to prevent suspension. Pinned tabs are never suspended. Pro users unlock unlimited whitelists and per-site timeout overrides.

**Why Tab Suspender?**

Browser tabs are memory hogs. With many tabs open, Chrome can consume 2-4GB+ of RAM, slowing your entire system. Tab Suspender lets you keep your workflow intact while dramatically reducing memory usage—without losing any tabs.

Unlike other tab managers, Tab Suspender is lightweight, fast, and respects your tab preferences. All your data stays on your computer (unless you enable Pro cloud backup).
